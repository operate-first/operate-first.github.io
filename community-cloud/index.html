Community Cloud

A Community Cloud is a shared environment for Open Source projects and SRE practitioners. An initial Community Cloud, which was established on bare metal hardware provided by the Mass Open Cloud (MOC), is documented here. It was decommissioned in the spring of 2023 due to lack of personnel to operate it. Open Source workloads have since moved to various community clusters to continue their work. At the present time, there is no bare metal cloud where Open Source operations can be vetted. Commitment of resources to manage such a cloud will be needed for resumption.
It's operated under a community SLA and as open and transparent as possible.
